Resources about GMC-4 Microcomputer

Most of the resources are in Japanese but Google Translate in Chrome does decent work instantly translating into English. The resources in English are marked.

GMC-4 is cloned from other microcomputers, and its instruction set is compatible with:

  • FX-microcomputer R-165
  • Tandy Microcomputer Trainer (english)
